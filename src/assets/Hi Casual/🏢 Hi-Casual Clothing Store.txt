🏢 Hi-Casual Clothing Store
📍 Location: Battaramulla
👤 Client: Mr. Priyantha
📅 Completion: 12th May 2012

📝 Description:
This commercial clothing store features a striking modern facade with full-height glass windows and a bold signage element, maximizing retail visibility. Located in a high-footfall shopping zone, the design emphasizes transparency and branding. The interior layout supports flexible merchandising while maintaining a clean, contemporary aesthetic suitable for fashion retail. The project was completed and handed over in May 2012
