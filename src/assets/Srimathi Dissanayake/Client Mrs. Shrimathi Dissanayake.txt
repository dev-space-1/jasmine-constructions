Client: Mrs. Shrimathi Dissanayake
Location: Mattegoda
Completion: 2012

This elegant private residence in Mattegoda blends contemporary living with traditional architectural elements, reflected in its finely detailed woodwork, ornate pillars, and open verandahs. Completed in 2012, the two-storey home is characterized by white-washed walls, a terracotta-toned roof, and carefully landscaped gardens that include a tranquil water feature. The project thoughtfully balances aesthetics and comfort, showcasing a refined residential design that celebrates spatial openness and natural light
